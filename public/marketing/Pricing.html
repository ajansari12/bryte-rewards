Questions

The things you'd ask over coffee.

Is there really no take-rate on rewards?
Really. When you load $10,000 into your rewards budget, $10,000 becomes available to your team. Gift cards redeem at face value. Charity donations go at par (the charity gets the full amount). We don't mark up the catalogue, we don't keep the breakage on expired points, and we don't sell your spending data. The only money Bryte Rewards makes is the per-seat subscription above.
How does the 30-day trial work?
Spin up a workspace for your whole team, not a demo sandbox. Invite everyone, import your values, post real recognition — it's your production account. If you don't convert within 30 days, we archive it and you walk away. If you do convert, nothing moves; your team just keeps using the same workspace they already love.
Can I mix seat types — e.g. corporate on Grow, frontline on Spark?
On Signature, yes — you can configure different feature sets for different populations (e.g. frontline gets the mobile-first experience, HQ gets full admin). On Spark and Grow, everyone in the workspace has the same tier. Most customers on 200+ seats land on Signature.
What's the billing minimum?
Ten seats. We're happy to work with teams of 10 on up. Below that, honestly, you probably don't need software — just say the nice thing in person or in your group chat. If you're at 6 and growing fast, reach out and we'll figure something out.
Do you offer non-profit or B-Corp discounts?
Yes. Registered Canadian charities get 40% off any tier. Certified B-Corps get 20% off Grow and Signature. Worker co-ops and Indigenous-led organisations get 50% off. Just let us know on the demo call — we'll apply it automatically, no forms to fill out.
Is Bryte Rewards GST/HST/QST-registered?
Yes — we're registered for GST, HST, and QST, and we collect and remit provincial sales tax on Canadian invoices. US customers get clean US invoices without Canadian tax. European customers get VAT-compliant invoices. EUR and USD billing are available on annual contracts.
Can I cancel mid-contract?
Annual contracts are the default — they're how we keep the per-seat price down. If you cancel mid-year we'll honour the remaining months; if you prefer monthly flexibility we offer month-to-month at +20% per seat. Either way, you own your data and can export everything on your way out.
How long does rollout actually take?
Most teams are live in an afternoon. Signature customers with SSO, HRIS sync, and custom rewards usually take 2–3 weeks end-to-end, including change management. The average time from kickoff to full company rollout across our customer base is 21 days.
Do you integrate with Humi, the Canadian HRIS?
We do. Native two-way sync on Grow and Signature. We also sync with BambooHR, Workday, ADP Workforce Now (Canada), UKG, and Rippling. If you're on something else — Ceridian Dayforce, Nethris, Payworks — we probably have it; ask.
Who actually uses Bryte Rewards day-to-day? Is this an HR tool?
HR buys it. Managers champion it. But the people who use it most are individual contributors — the server, the electrician, the nurse, the junior associate. That's the whole point. If it only works for HR, it's not really working.
