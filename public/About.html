About us

We make the tool we wished we'd had at our last jobs.

Bryte Rewards is a small, Toronto-based team building recognition software that feels like your company wrote it — not a vendor. We're employee #7 at heart: the person who notices the thing and wants to say something, but the form is three screens long and the reward is a badge.

The manifesto

Recognition should sound like you wrote it. Not like HR approved it. Not like a vendor templated it. Not like a bonus was automatically triggered by a points engine. The real thing, in your voice, to a real person — and somehow we keep building software that gets in the way of that.

So we're building the opposite.

— Nadia & Jordan, co-founders

What we believe

Six principles we keep taped to the wall.

01

Words beat points.

Points run out. A sentence someone meant never does. We optimize for the sentence first, the points second.

02

Recognition is not performance.

It isn't review bait or a manager checkbox. We keep it separate from performance workflows on purpose.

03

Made in Canada, on purpose.

Your data lives in Canada. Your catalogue is priced in CAD. Your workflow respects Quebec's rules. Not an afterthought.

04

Honest pricing.

One number per person per month. Everything included. No SSO tax, no integration tax, no "Enterprise Plus" tier.

05

Slow software.

We ship quietly, rewrite often, and don't chip away at your attention with growth-hacked nonsense.

06

Your words, your tool.

We provide the surface. Your company's values, voice, and traditions do the rest. Never the other way around.
