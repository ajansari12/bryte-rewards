02 · Values

The words you stand for, right in the software.

Tell Bryte Rewards what matters. Every recognition tags a value. Over time, you see what your culture actually looks like — not what a survey said six months ago.

  • Industry packsEight pre-built starter kits: trades, hospitality, healthcare, retail, and more.
  • Point weightsSafety counts differently than Fun. You decide — per value, per location.
  • Rename, retire, rewriteYour values aren't carved in stone — and neither is Bryte Rewards's.
  • Bilingual by defaultEvery value label has FR out of the box, editable for regional phrasing.

03 · Rewards

Real rewards. Zero take.

Points become things people actually want. Gift cards, team lunches, days off, a custom hoodie from the shop down the street. Your budget, your catalogue — we take zero cut.

  • Canadian catalogueTim Hortons, SAQ, Indigo, Canadian Tire, MEC, La Baie, MLSE, Home Depot CA — plus regional favourites.
  • Build your ownAdd company swag, paid time off, charity donations, team experiences. Anything you can mail or book.
  • Manager budgetsCap spend per team, per quarter. No surprises on the invoice.
  • Tax-awareWe flag CRA-reportable amounts and export to your payroll.

04 · Dashboards

Numbers that lead somewhere.

Not vanity scores. Signal: which values are alive, which teams are quiet, whose managers aren't giving — or receiving. Export-ready for your next all-hands.

  • Culture pulseOne-glance health: participation, balance across values, and recognition latency.
  • Manager lensSee your team's patterns — who you've recognised, who you haven't, and what they'd value.
  • Flags, not scoresSoft nudges when something looks off. Never a leaderboard of shame.
  • Export anywhereCSV, PDF, and email digest. Plays nicely with BI tools.
